The Grudge

Matthew Williams (William Mapother), his wife, Jennifer (Clea DuVall),
and mother, Emma (Grace Zabriskie), are Americans making a new
life in Tokyo. Together they move into a house that has been
the site of supernatural occurrences in the past, and it isn't
long before their new home begins terrorizing the Williams family
as well. The house, as it turns out, is the site of a curse that
lingers in a specific place and claims the lives of anyone that
comes near.
  • Release date: October 27, 2004 (Philippines)
  • Director: Takashi Shimizu
  • Film series: The Grudge
  • Adapted from: Ju-On: The Grudge
  • Producers: Sam Raimi, Robert Tapert, Takashige Ichise
